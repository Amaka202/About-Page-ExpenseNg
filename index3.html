About us

Mission

Building a more Transparent Government

ExpenseNG's main goal is to show all Expenditure Data of the Nigerian Government to theNigerian public, the Monthly and Yearly Governmentexpenditure. All expenditures from the Congressional appropriation to Federal Agencies and down to the Local Communities and Businesses.

Background

A continuing effort

The Federal Funding and Transperency Act of 2006 (FFATA) was signed into law on september 26, 2006.The legislation required that federal contract,grant, loan and other financial assistance awards of more than 9 Million be displayed on a publicly accessible and searchable website to give the Nigerian public access to information on how tax in Naira are being spent.

The transperency efforts of FFATA were explained with the Digital Accountability and Transperency Act, this is why ExpenseNG came into being, disclosing federal Expenditure and improving the quality of Data submitted by Federal Agencies holding them accountable.

How it Works

Data Sources

Connecting the dots across Government, linking data from a variety of of government systems to improve transparency on federal spending to the public.Data is uploaded directly from more than hundreds federal agencies’ financial systems

Data Quality

A commitment to accuracy and completeness.ExpenseNG is commited in improving the accuracy and completeness of the data using data validatidation before being published to the general public

Data Release

ExpenseNG is developed using various technological methods, Our current release approach is a two-week development sprint, followed by a two- week testing period, followed by public release.
